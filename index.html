About Polar Bears

Polar bears are large carnivorous mammals native to the Arctic region. They are excellent swimmers and primarily inhabit the sea ice throughout the Arctic Ocean. These majestic creatures are well-adapted to their frigid environment.

Polar bears are listed as vulnerable due to the ongoing loss of their sea ice habitat caused by climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect these incredible animals and their ecosystem.
